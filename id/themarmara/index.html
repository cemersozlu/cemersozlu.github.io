The Marmara Taksim

2013 Interior

A design collaboration with Architect Albert Abut. A result of 2 months of research on contemporization of traditional Ottoman patterns. A truly unique blend of contemporary yet traditional design by updating an untouched style for years. Project consists of 2 different patterns each for specific purposes, a layer offering a new perspective between Istanbul and you and one alternating 3D produced walls surrounds and welcomes you to a completely unique yet traditional environment you never experienced before.
