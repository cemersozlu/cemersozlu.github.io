Born in Bursa Turkey, 1979. Successfully graduated from Istanbul Bilgi University, Visual Communication Design department at 2001. Got his MSc degree from GITS Media Lab, Waseda University, Tokyo in 2004. Stayed in Tokyo for years and worked in different design companies in various positions such as senior CG designer, art director. Worked in Istanbul Bilgi University, Visual Communication Design department full-time academic between 2006 - 2019. Gave courses about branding design, UI/UX design and graphic design. To date, worked on a wide range of projects, from startups to large-scale UI/UX solutions and branding initiatives.

Passionate about researching potential future UI/UX technologies and possibilities with emerging technologies. Currently working as senior product Designer, freelance designer, mentor in various incubation centers about branding and user experience, consultant for various companies and startups in Tokyo, Istanbul and Toronto.
